Welcome to the HALLOWEENTOWN Site Plan

Site Purpose

The Halloweentown Chamber is a multi-creature neighborhood organization that enthusiastically facilitates, educates, and advocates on behalf of its inhabitants through our services and programs. We are proud to offer numerous networking opportunities and events around Town Square for both mortals and creatures alike, educational seminars at our esteemed Witch University, and special offers to our Movie Theater and Halloween Health Spa. Nothing is impossible in Halloweentown! For more information, please reach out by cauldron.

Scenarios

Culture and tourism: How many types of creatures live in Halloweentown, and what they are? What cultures are there?

History and politics: How was Halloweentown founded? Who ruled over it before is was a town?

Trading and economy: What is the economy in Halloweentown? What kind of trading occurs in Halloweentown?
